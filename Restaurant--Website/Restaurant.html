History of Chawla's

Born in UP, brought up in Punjab - probably this is how one can sum up the Chawla's Chicken. It all started one day at Haldwani in District Nainital. Late S. Attar Singh Chawla was trying to improve upon one of his favorites recipes in the restaurants in his free time when he ended up with the one Chawla's Cream Chicken.
